# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Refugee and host communities, people seeking asylum, Ugandan public institutions, humanitarian organisations, grant makers, volunteers, researchers and members of the public. Visitors need a trustworthy, accessible view of IRI's purpose, planned programmes and responsible ways to engage.

## Product Purpose

Present Ineza Refugees Initiative as a credible Ugandan institution, distinguish proposed work from verified achievements, and create clear pathways to learn, partner, contact and register support interest.

## Brand Personality

Grounded, sheltering and hopeful. Calm institutional confidence without pity, spectacle or inflated claims.

## Anti-references

Generic technology startup pages, gaming-style 3D, AI charity templates, pity-based fundraising, fake impact counters, glossy glass-card grids and restless animation.

## Design Principles

- Dignity before drama.
- State what is known and label what is planned.
- Let real thematic imagery carry emotion without implying beneficiary identity.
- Make institutional accountability visible, not buried.
- Use motion to clarify narrative progression, never to obstruct access.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA, keyboard and screen-reader access, robust focus management, reduced-motion alternatives, readable overlays, 44px touch targets and performance suitable for lower-cost mobile devices and slower connections.
