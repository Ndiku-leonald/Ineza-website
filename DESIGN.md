# Design System

## Intent

An immersive humanitarian brand surface inspired by a shaded community gathering place: deep forest surrounds full-bleed photography, warm cream creates reading space, and terracotta and gold mark human action.

## Foundations

- Palette: `#173F35`, `#0E2D26`, `#F7F0E5`, `#FFF9F1`, `#5B3924`, `#B76E46`, `#D8E3D8`, `#D9C6AA`, `#D6A84B`, `#241A14`, white.
- Typography: Inter for interface and prose; Instrument Serif italic only for selected emotional phrases in major headings.
- Radius: full-pill navigation and action controls; 16px programme surfaces; 28px only for the hero-to-content overlap.
- Motion: one cinematic hero entrance, restrained pointer parallax, scroll-revealed programme sequence, 200–700ms ease-out. All disabled or reduced under `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Layout: full-viewport hero, floating navigation, central narrative about section, sticky programme index beside vertically paced programme stories, split accountability and direct closing CTA.

## Content and Media

Photographs are thematic samples pending copyright, consent and caption approval. They never represent IRI participants or completed work. Programme status remains visible inside every programme story.
